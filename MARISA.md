## Safejumps

## Gameplan Notes
- Don't backdash after `Superman Punch`!!! `Drive Rush` catches your backdash.

## Gladius
- Whiff punish with `stHK` or `stHP`
- `Drive Reversal` punishes all versions of it, both uncharged and charged.
  - Big meter cost, though.
